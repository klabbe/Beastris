Delete Your BeastBlocks Account

Last updated: April 2026

You can delete your BeastBlocks account and all associated data at any time using either of the methods below.

⚠️ Account deletion is permanent and irreversible. The following data will be erased:
  • Your e-mail address and password
  • Your profile (alias, name, country)
Your leaderboard scores will be anonymised — the scores remain on the global leaderboard but are permanently unlinked from your account and identity. Local game data (scores stored on your device) is not affected.

Option 1 — Delete directly in the app (instant)

1. Open BeastBlocks and sign in.
2. Tap your alias / profile icon in the top right corner of the screen.
3. In the Profile dialog, tap Delete Account (bottom left, red text).
4. Confirm in the prompt. Your account and all data are deleted immediately.

Option 2 — Request deletion by e-mail

If you no longer have access to the app, send an e-mail to beastris@alskami.se with the subject "Account Deletion Request" and the e-mail address associated with your account. We will process the request within 30 days.
